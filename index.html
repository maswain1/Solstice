SOLSTICE

All for a Starry Night

Bio

Solstice is a progressive rock and metal instrumentalist from Birmingham, Alabama. Some of the artists he takes most influence from include Periphery, The Weeknd, Intervals, Skyharbor, Plini, and Purity Ring. Shortly before releasing his debut single, "For What It's Worth...", Solstice released a guitar cover of The Weeknd's song, Privilege.
Solstice made his debut through the record, All for a Starry Night, which was released on September 14th, 2018.

All for a Starry Night (2018)

All for a Starry Night is Solstice's debut record. Featuring eight original tracks and two guitar remixes (which are both free to download here), All for a Starry Night drags the listener through emotional ambiguity, elation, and ultimately, down into a dark abyss during the last track of the record, "All for a Starry Night" (which features guest vocals by Teddy Muciri). The record's promotion was conducted exclusively through his Instagram where Solstice released several teasers and guitar playthroughs of his work to give listeners a preview of music to come. On August 17th, 2018, Solstice's debut single, "For What It's Worth...", was released exclusively on his Soundcloud and garnered several plays within minutes of its release.
